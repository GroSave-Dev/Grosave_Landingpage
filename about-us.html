Who Are We?

We are a team of young leaders who are passionate about building and launching the GroSave vision. We believe in teamwork and are working hard to bring the idea of GroSave to the commercial space.

We have undertaken plenty of research to analyze multitudinous aspects of food wastage and are designing the app accordingly. Added to the utterly dedicated team, we also have highly experienced industry professionals who impart us their indispensible pieces of advice and guide us through this stimulating journey.

Vision made possible with support from Space Kidz India and Hexaware
