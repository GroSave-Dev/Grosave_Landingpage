    Our Mission

    The concept of GroSave originated with the intention of eradicating domestic food wastage. We are not only aiming to minimize food wastage but also helping to channelize surplus food to the needy. GroSave utilizes the best available technology to bring this distinctive idea into existence.

    GroSave is a comprehensive app which would enable you to manage all your groceries at one place right with the help of your smartphone. We are striving to equip the app with an efficient record management system where all your grocery information is stored and you get notified periodically before your food products reach the expiration threshold. You can either consume or donate them to the needy before they go past the expiry dates.

    The statistics indicating food wastage, especially at the domestic level, would blow your mind! This is the motivation for GroSave So, we are putting our earnest efforts to make this a successful mission.
